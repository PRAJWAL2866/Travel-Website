Spiti Valley, a Himalayan gem, offers breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and serene villages like Kaza and Tabo. Known for its rugged beauty and adventure, it’s best visited in summer (May-September) for trekking, stargazing, and spiritual tranquility.

Chandratal Lake, the "Moon Lake" of Himachal Pradesh, is a pristine alpine gem surrounded by towering peaks. Known for its crystal-clear waters and mesmerizing reflections, it’s best visited in summer (June-September) for camping, trekking, and serene beauty.
